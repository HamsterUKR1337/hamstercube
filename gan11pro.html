Gan 11 m pro

PRO PERFORMANCE AT THE CORE - Meet GAN’s latest 3x3 magnetic cube flagship, the GAN 11 M Pro. This 11th-generation cube not only introduces a new naming convention but also signals a shift in the cubing world. It features an advanced Core Magnet System, elevating speed cube stability to an unprecedented level.
REVOLUTIONARY MAGNETIC CORE - The future of cube design is here with the magnetic core positioning system! In the GAN 11 M Pro, magnets are strategically placed between the core and the corner pieces. This design ensures consistent magnetic alignment during turns, providing a seamless blend of speed and stability. You'll experience unmatched precision and control from the very first twist.
LIGHTWEIGHT, POWERFUL PERFORMANCE - When it comes to speed cubing, lightweight is key. The 3x3 magnetic cube weighs just 63 grams, offering incredible control and fluidity. Available in three surface finishes—Glossy, Frosted, and Rubberized—each option provides its own unique advantage. Plus, there’s a special frosted version with black internals for those seeking a standout choice.
FULLY CUSTOMIZABLE - GAN continues to lead in customization with the 11 M Pro. You can fine-tune the cube to your preference with 7 levels of magnet strength, 4 nut distance options, and 6 elasticity settings. With 168 possible configurations, this cube offers unparalleled versatility. Step-by-step instructions are included for easy setup.
JOIN THE GAN FAMILY - By choosing GAN, you’re aligning with the world’s top 3x3 cube brand. Stay up-to-date with new products, connect with other fans, and even influence future designs. Rest assured, our dedicated Customer Service Team is here to ensure you have everything you need for an exceptional experience.
