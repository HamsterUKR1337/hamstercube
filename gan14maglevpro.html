Gan 14 maglev pro

Enhanced Precision and Stability - The GAN14 Pro takes performance to a new level, evolving from its predecessor with refined edge pieces for superior stability and flawless corner alignment. Its advanced center-locking technology prevents popping, while an extended center cap latch ensures secure positioning. With a simple one-touch lid opening tool, adjustments are easier and smoother than ever.
Consistent, Controlled Feel - Featuring Enhanced Core Positioning 4.0, the GAN14 Pro’s axis structure provides uniform force distribution through its magnetic core, achieving an unmatched balance of stability and control. With 1296 configurations, each twist can be tailored to the perfect hand feel.
Magnetic Levitation Technology - This cube is equipped with an advanced magnetic suspension system, offering 12 customizable magnet settings between the corners and core. Thanks to magnetic repulsion, the GAN14 Maglev cube can auto-align within 15-40 degrees of a completed turn, virtually eliminating friction for a near-levitating experience.
Precision Tuning with Dual Adjustment - Easily fine-tune your cube with the magnetic GES v3, which provides 6 tension and 6 center travel options. This dual numerical system allows you to craft a cubing experience that’s entirely your own, offering a wide range of possibilities in one compact design.
A Trusted Name in Cubing - Choosing GAN means joining a legacy of excellence in the speed cube world. Stay updated on the latest products, connect with fans, and share your thoughts on future models. With GAN’s responsive Customer Service Team, every experience is designed to satisfy.
